How We Use Podcasts

The Super Times is currently available as a subscription based iPhone app. The subscription we charge is for the technology and weekly content recommendations not the content itself. This is similar to numerous Podcatching services and software.

If you are a podcast maker or publisher and do not wish your programmes to be featured in the Super Times then please get in touch with us at content@thesupertimes.com
